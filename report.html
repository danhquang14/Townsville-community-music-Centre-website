Mission

The main goal of this website concentrates on recruiting people and engaging people to participate in our activities such as promoting concerts and workshops, volunteering for administration to have a large community on the web together with advertising the useful services of our website to over the world. For concerts, the other target which our group is looking forward to is to increase ticket sales, especially to the public. Because the social media is very popular nowadays, our group takes this advantage to create the website to let people know more about this site. The last goal of the website is to encourage performers to let us organize a concert for them. Furthermore, our group wants to encourage performers to cooperate with promotion, photo shoots, media releases, etc. that will make the website more and more popular in the world
